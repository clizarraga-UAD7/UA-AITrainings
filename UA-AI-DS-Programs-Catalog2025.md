## **KEY INSIGHTS**

1. **Dominant Focus**: College of Information Science leads with 35% of all programs
2. **Graduate-Heavy**: 70% of programs are at the graduate level (MS, certificates, PhD)
3. **Data Science Emphasis**: Data Science is the most common focus area (7 programs)
4. **Flexible Delivery**: 56.5% of programs offer online or hybrid options
5. **Interdisciplinary Approach**: Programs span 6 different colleges/departments
6. **Stackable Credentials**: Strong emphasis on certificates that ladder into degrees
7. **Professional Programs**: Multiple programs designed for working professionals
8. **International Reach**: Global partnerships expand access to international students
