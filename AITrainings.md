## Summary of Findings

The search identified **24 distinct AI-related training programs, certificates, and workshop series** offered by the University of Arizona in 2024-2025, distributed across multiple colleges and departments:

- **Continuing & Professional Education**: 3 major certificate/bootcamp programs
- **Data Science Institute**: 7 workshop series and programs
- **University Libraries**: 6 workshop series
- **College/Department Collaborations**: 8 specialized programs and series

The training offerings range from beginner-friendly introductions to advanced technical workshops, covering diverse applications including:
- Prompt engineering and AI automation
- Machine learning and deep learning
- Large language models and generative AI
- AI in teaching and learning
- AI for public health
- AI in language education
- AI ethics and literacy

Most workshops are offered free of charge, while certificate programs through Continuing & Professional Education are fee-based but offer various payment options and discounts.
