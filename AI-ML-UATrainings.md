## Summary of Expanded Findings

The expanded search identified **65 distinct ML/DL/NN/NLP-focused training programs, certificates, and workshop series** offered by the University of Arizona in 2024-2025.

### Key ML/DL/NN/NLP Training Categories:

**Deep Learning & Neural Networks:**
- PyTorch fundamentals and deep learning from scratch
- Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) for computer vision
- Recurrent Neural Networks (RNNs) for sequence modeling
- Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs)
- Transformer architectures and attention mechanisms

**Natural Language Processing:**
- Comprehensive NLP workshop series (10 weeks)
- NLP with spaCy, PyTorch, and scikit-learn
- Text pre-processing and feature engineering
- Named entity recognition and dependency parsing
- Graduate and undergraduate NLP certificates (formal programs)

**Machine Learning Fundamentals:**
- Classical ML algorithms (supervised/unsupervised learning)
- Decision trees, SVMs, linear regression
- Ensemble methods and model evaluation
- Feature engineering and hyperparameter tuning
- ML model deployment and scaling

**Specialized Applications:**
- Computer vision and image classification
- Geospatial deep learning for aerial imagery
- Multimodal learning (text, image, audio)
- Large Language Models and generative AI
- Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)
- LangChain and AI application development

**Tools & Frameworks Covered:**
- PyTorch, TensorFlow, Keras
- Scikit-learn, Pandas, NumPy
- Hugging Face Transformers
- spaCy, NLTK
- Ollama, LangChain, Gradio
- CyVerse, Docker containers

**Format Distribution:**
- **Short workshops:** 1-2 hours (beginner-friendly introductions)
- **Multi-week series:** 3-10 weeks (comprehensive coverage)
- **Intensive bootcamps:** 9+ months (career-focused programs)
- **Summer schools:** 4-5 days (intensive training)
- **Certificates:** 9 credit hours (formal academic credentials)

Most workshops are offered **free of charge**, while bootcamps and formal certificate programs are fee-based with various payment options, scholarships, and employer reimbursement available.
